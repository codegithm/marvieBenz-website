MARVIE

The Life Of Kil'Moves

In an era where his peers are mostly ‘Trap-bound’, Marvie Benz has been proving himself to possess an understanding of music far beyond sound and genres. His 2018 release — In The Valley EP — is testament to this. The homie is just getting started and is really here to stay! Born and bred in the northern KZN town of Ulundi , Marvie clearly has no bounds in the visions he has for himself and his brand. ‘Life of Kil’moves‘ is the title to the homie’s latest extended play, a solid four-track project executed with the highest precision. The sonic make-up of the project distinguishes the artist from a lot of what’s in the market currently, as he intentionally went for a more ‘traditional’ Boom Bap sound; something that’s hard to find these days (especially among young Hip Hop heads). The artist tackles a lot of pressing issues, from personal frustrations stemming from the urge to achieve one’s goals (as painted vividly on the track ‘Bitter‘), to voicing out his concerns about things that we all sort of go through as young people (as relayed on the track ‘Damned‘). “Every other day/ Fighting demons like a pastor/ Nigguhs outchea thinking that/ They live a better life/ Acting like the grass/ Is greener on the other side/ I don’t believe in small talk/ Rather see it with my eyes” Track 3 of the EP is titled ‘Baby Daddy‘, and Marvie recounts the “Boy Meets Girl” narrative through his own experiences. This particular track is constructed in such a way that it gives the listener the impression that it’s two tracks, an effect created by how the sound of the track switches up towards the end (turning the track somewhat into a ballad of sorts). Marvie Benz possesses an incredibly crazy work ethic, which is what will help get him where he needs to be. His music is super listener-friendly, and would give most listeners the impression that they’ve heard it before. If you haven’t, do check out the homie’s project. ‘Life of Kil’moves’ is available on all major online streaming platforms, including Apple Music, Deezer and Spotify; get acquainted with good music!
